## S1 - Existing Shopify B2B / Plus Merchants

- **Persona:** Emma - Shopify B2B Lead
- **Role:** E-commerce Manager / Head of Digital
- **Company:** Mid to upper-mid merchant (0.5M-20M B2B/year), Shopify Plus with Companies & Locations. Vertical: wholesale distributor, manufacturer, F&B, industrial.
- **Main goals:** Scale B2B revenue, reduce manual work, replace old systems, provide proper portal for sales and buyers.
- **KPIs:** B2B revenue, AOV, repeat order rate, self-service order ratio, onboarding time for B2B accounts, support tickets about B2B checkout/quotes.
- **Daily pains:** Native Shopify B2B not enough for real complexity; spreadsheet/email quotes; too many B2B apps; slow sales workflows; IT resistance to custom dev.
- **Typical objections:** Native Shopify only? Performance impact? Maintenance complexity? ERP compatibility?
- **Success looks like:** 80%+ B2B orders via self-service portal; fewer manual quotes; unified pricing/discount logic; one admin place for customer and rules.
- **Messaging hooks:**
  - Extend Shopify B2B into a full wholesale operations hub.
  - Centralize pricing, quotes, and access around Companies & Locations.
  - One Suite instead of 4-5 plugins.

## S2 - Hybrid Merchants (B2B + B2C in One Store)

- **Persona:** Lucas - DTC Brand Owner Turning to Wholesale
- **Role:** Founder / Ecom Manager
- **Company:** SMB-mid (0.2M-10M/year), strong B2C, growing wholesale in one Shopify store. Verticals: fashion, beauty, home, F&B.
- **Main goals:** Keep one storefront/brand, smooth wholesale UX, avoid second site, keep stack simple.
- **KPIs:** Wholesale revenue share, active wholesale accounts, average wholesale order size, B2B reorder frequency, wholesale support tickets.
- **Daily pains:** Public vs hidden pricing, MOQ/MOV logic conflicts, too many discount apps, B2B buyers confused by B2C UX, hacked order forms.
- **Typical objections:** Will B2C checkout break? Too heavy for small team? Can launch fast?
- **Success looks like:** Public B2C normal; wholesale buyers login to own prices/collections; simple quick order; minimums automatic; less admin manual work.
- **Messaging hooks:**
  - Run B2C and B2B in one Shopify store without chaos.
  - Dedicated B2B portal after login while keeping DTC simple.
  - Replace 3-4 wholesale apps with one B2B Suite.

## S3 - Migrating from Legacy B2B Systems

- **Persona:** Robert - Digital Transformation Director
- **Role:** CIO / IT Director / Digital Transformation Lead
- **Company:** Mid-enterprise (1M-50M+ B2B/year), migrating from Magento/custom/on-prem. Verticals: industrial, automotive parts, electronics, B2B food service.
- **Main goals:** Replatform to Shopify, reduce tech debt/TCO, preserve critical workflows, modern UX, stable ERP integration.
- **KPIs:** Go-live timing, migration risk, uptime, TCO, key-account adoption, NPS of top customers, integration stability.
- **Daily pains:** Legacy system expensive/slow, complex pricing/catalogs, internal fear that Shopify is only B2C, many edge cases, pressure to modernize safely.
- **Typical objections:** Can Shopify + apps match current features? Who owns risk? Security/scalability? ERP/CRM integration quality?
- **Success looks like:** Shopify B2B portal live, key accounts active daily, fewer custom dev requests, suite centralizes pricing/access/quotes, stable integrations.
- **Messaging hooks:**
  - Turn Shopify into a full B2B portal, not just retail.
  - Match/exceed legacy workflows with modern SaaS suite.
  - Centralize complex pricing and quoting with clean integrations.

## S4 - Agencies & Shopify Experts (Implementers)

- **Persona:** Sarah - Shopify B2B Solutions Architect
- **Role:** Agency founder / Solutions architect / Senior Shopify dev
- **Company:** Shopify agency/SI with 2-10 B2B projects/year, Plus merchants, multi-store, multi-region integrations.
- **Main goals:** Standard reliable B2B stack, lower project risk, faster delivery, avoid vendor friction, improve margin.
- **KPIs:** Delivery time, implementation margin, bug/change-request volume, client satisfaction, repeat projects, partner revenue.
- **Daily pains:** Re-researching apps, juggling many vendors, poor app support/docs, agency blamed for vendor issues, hard-to-explain stacks.
- **Typical objections:** Covers 80% of B2B use cases? Support speed? Partner pricing/benefits?
- **Success looks like:** Reusable B2B blueprint, standard documentation, fewer surprises, strong vendor relationship, expert positioning.
- **Messaging hooks:**
  - Standardize B2B builds with one proven suite.
  - One vendor, one roadmap, one support team.
  - Faster delivery, fewer risks, better margins.

## S5 - New-to-B2B Shopify Merchants

- **Persona:** Anna - Growing DTC Founder Exploring Wholesale
- **Role:** Founder / Solo Ecom Manager
- **Company:** Growing DTC Shopify brand (0.1M-5M/year), mostly B2C, receiving wholesale inquiries, limited resources.
- **Main goals:** Launch credible wholesale quickly, keep operations simple, avoid complex enterprise tools.
- **KPIs:** Wholesale accounts created, first wholesale orders, B2B revenue share, manual effort, onboarding speed.
- **Daily pains:** Email/manual invoices, no registration/pricing portal, fear of breaking B2C, limited B2B knowledge/time.
- **Typical objections:** Too advanced? Need full suite or one app? Setup too complicated?
- **Success looks like:** Fast wholesale launch, buyers can register/login/see pricing/order, minimal manual work, unchanged B2C flow.
- **Messaging hooks:**
  - Easiest way to start wholesale on existing Shopify store.
  - Start simple, scale into full suite later.
  - Launch a professional wholesale portal in days, not months.
