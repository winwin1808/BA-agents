## Company Background

**Company:** BSS Commerce  
**Domain:** eCommerce solutions (apps + services)  
**Core focus:** Shopify
**Primary market:** Larger and SMB-midmarket merchants, with B2B or hybrid B2C+B2B models

## Mission & Vision

**Mission:** Help B2B merchants run wholesale on Shopify quickly, flexibly, and in a scalable way.  
**Vision:** Become the default B2B Suite for Shopify merchants in the SMB and mid-market segments.

### Product Values

- **Merchant-first:** Optimized for real operational problems merchants face.
- **Modular but integrated:** Each module can stand alone but works smoothly as part of the suite.
- **Fast time-to-value:** Install fast, configure fast, see value within the first week.
- **Transparent pricing logic:** Clear pricing rules that avoid rule conflicts.
- **Scalable operations:** Support scaling from a few B2B customers to thousands of accounts.

## Strategic Context: B2B Suite

BSS Commerce B2B Suite focuses on 5 capability groups:

1. **Customer Access & Segmentation**

   - Lock/hide price based on customer tags or login state
   - Registration forms and approval flows for B2B accounts
   - Buyer permissions by group

1. **B2B Pricing & Catalog Control**

   - Price lists per customer group
   - Tier pricing / quantity breaks
   - MOQ, pack size, checkout constraints
   - Catalog visibility per customer segment

1. **Order Workflows**

   - Quick order forms and fast reorder
   - Draft orders and sales-assisted checkout
   - Request for Quote (RFQ) + quote negotiation

1. **Payment & Terms**

   - Net payment terms (Net 15/30/60)
   - Partial payment/deposit
   - Tax/VAT handling for B2B scenarios

1. **Integrations & Operations**

   - Sync with ERP/CRM/email via API or middleware
   - Audit trail for changes in prices/policies
   - Reporting for B2B revenue and conversion funnels

## Target Market

### Primary ICP

- Shopify merchants with stable revenue who are starting a wholesale channel
- Distributors/manufacturers who need an ordering portal for dealers
- D2C brands expanding into B2B with specific pricing/terms requirements

### Customer Segment Map

Use `./references/CUSTOMER_SEGMENTS.md` when the ask needs persona detail, objections, or messaging hooks. Keep this section as the lightweight segment map:

- **S1 - Existing Shopify B2B / Plus Merchants:** already running B2B on Shopify/Plus and looking to scale operations, self-service, and suite cohesion.
- **S2 - Hybrid Merchants (B2B + B2C in One Store):** want one storefront, simpler stack, and clean separation between public B2C and logged-in wholesale experiences.
- **S3 - Migrating from Legacy B2B Systems:** need Shopify to replace Magento/custom systems without losing pricing, quoting, or integration depth.
- **S4 - Agencies & Shopify Experts:** implementation partners optimizing for repeatable delivery, lower project risk, and a standard B2B blueprint.
- **S5 - New-to-B2B Shopify Merchants:** early wholesale adopters who need fast launch, low complexity, and a safe path from one app to the full suite.

## Key Terms

- **Merchant Admin:** Person configuring B2B logic in Shopify admin/app admin
- **B2B Buyer:** Business customer placing orders under an approved account
- **Customer Segment:** Group of customers to which pricing, catalog, and terms rules are applied
- **Price List:** Dedicated price table for a segment
- **RFQ:** Request for Quote, the process of requesting and negotiating a quote
- **Net Terms:** Post-paid payment terms (e.g., Net 15/30/60) after an order is placed
